Welcome to DroperX, the ultimate solution for fast, secure, and hassle-free file sharing. Our mission is to break down the barriers of data transfer, providing a seamless experience across all your devices.

Peer-to-Peer Architecture

DroperX establishes a direct WebRTC connection between your devices. No middlemen, no cloud storage limits, just pure unthrottled speed.

Zero Data Retention

Your files are never uploaded to a centralized server. The moment you close the app, your session is permanently destroyed. True privacy by design.

Cross-Platform Seamlessness

Whether you're on a desktop, tablet, or mobile phone, DroperX adapts instantly. Scan a QR code or enter a Room ID to start transferring instantly.
