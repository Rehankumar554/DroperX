The Future of
File Transfer.

Welcome to DroperX. Our mission is to break down the barriers of data transfer by providing a seamless, limitless, and highly secure experience across all your devices.

router

Peer-to-Peer Architecture

DroperX completely eliminates the middleman. By utilizing advanced WebRTC protocols, we establish a direct, unthrottled connection between the sender and the receiver. No cloud storage limits, no artificial speed caps—just pure, direct transfer.

security

Zero Data Retention

Your privacy is our highest priority. Files are never uploaded to any centralized server or database. The data flows directly between the two connected devices, and the moment you close the tab, the session and all its data are permanently destroyed.

devices

Cross-Platform Seamlessness

Whether you're sharing files between a Windows desktop, a Mac, an iPad, or an Android phone, DroperX adapts instantly. Simply scan a QR code or share a 6-digit Room ID to start transferring instantly across any modern web browser.
