Frequently Asked
Questions.

How does DroperX work?

DroperX uses WebRTC technology to establish a direct Peer-to-Peer (P2P) connection between your device and the receiver's device. Files are transferred directly between you two without ever touching a centralized storage server.

Are my files stored on your servers?

No. We do not have any cloud storage or databases for your files. Our servers only facilitate the initial handshake (signaling) to connect the two devices. Once connected, the transfer happens directly between the peers.

Is there a file size limit?

Because the transfer happens directly between devices, there are no artificial file size limits. However, extremely large files (e.g., over 10GB) might depend on your browser's memory and connection stability.

Is the transfer secure?

Yes. All WebRTC data channels are automatically End-to-End Encrypted (E2EE) using DTLS and SRTP. Even we cannot see the contents of what you are transferring.

What happens if I close the tab during a transfer?

Because it's a real-time direct connection, closing the tab or disconnecting from the internet will immediately stop the transfer and the connection will be lost. Both devices must keep the tab open until the transfer completes.
