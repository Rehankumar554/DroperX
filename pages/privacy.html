Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026. At DroperX, privacy isn't just a promise; it is hardcoded into our architecture.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Because DroperX is a Peer-to-Peer (P2P) file transfer protocol, we do not collect, intercept, route, or store any of the files you transfer. We do not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or verify your identity.

2. How Transfers Work

When you create a room, DroperX acts solely as a signaling server to exchange connection coordinates (SDP and ICE candidates) between you and your peer. Once the connection is established, the signaling server steps out of the way. Your files are transferred directly between your device and the receiving device over WebRTC data channels.

3. End-to-End Encryption

All data sent through DroperX is inherently encrypted end-to-end using standard WebRTC security protocols (DTLS and SRTP). No third party, including DroperX, can decrypt or view the contents of your transfer.

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use strictly necessary local storage mechanisms (such as standard session storage) to maintain your connection state while the app is open. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or invasive analytics tools to profile our users.
