Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026. At DroperX, privacy isn't just a promise; it is hardcoded into our architecture.

1. Zero Data Collection Architecture

DroperX is structurally engineered around a "Privacy by Design" and "Zero-Knowledge" framework. We explicitly do not collect, route, log, or store any of the file payloads you transfer. The platform operates without user accounts, eliminating the need to harvest Personally Identifiable Information (PII) such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers. Your identity and the contents of your transfers remain entirely invisible to RKS Development Studio.

2. The Role of the Signaling Server

When you initiate a room on DroperX, our infrastructure acts solely as a signaling broker (utilizing PeerJS and MQTT protocols). The server's only function is to exchange volatile connection metadata—specifically Session Description Protocol (SDP) and Interactive Connectivity Establishment (ICE) candidates—between the sending and receiving devices. Once the direct WebRTC peer-to-peer (P2P) link is established, the signaling server is completely bypassed. This metadata is ephemeral and is permanently purged from memory immediately upon connection establishment or timeout.

3. End-to-End Encryption (E2EE) Mechanisms

All file transfers executed via DroperX's WebRTC data channels are protected by mandatory, military-grade End-to-End Encryption. We utilize Datagram Transport Layer Security (DTLS) for secure key exchange and Secure Real-time Transport Protocol (SRTP) for data encryption. Consequently, any data traversing the network is cryptographically scrambled and can only be decrypted by the intended recipient device. DroperX does not possess, generate, or manage the encryption keys, rendering it technologically impossible for us to intercept or decrypt your files.

4. Strictly Necessary Local Storage

To provide a seamless User Experience (UX), DroperX utilizes your browser's native `localStorage` and `sessionStorage` strictly for maintaining application state. This includes caching basic UI preferences (such as "Don't show again" warnings) and ephemeral session IDs. We strictly prohibit the use of third-party tracking cookies, invasive marketing pixels, or behavioral profiling analytics. Your local storage data never leaves your device.

5. Global Privacy Rights (GDPR, CCPA, CPRA)

Because DroperX does not collect or process Personally Identifiable Information (PII) or user files, the traditional obligations of a "Data Controller" or "Data Processor" under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) are inherently satisfied by our technical architecture. There is no user data to request, rectify, or delete, as no user data is retained on our servers.

6. Policy Modifications

RKS Development Studio reserves the right to amend this Privacy Policy. Any material changes to our data handling practices will be reflected on this page. Because we do not collect contact information, we cannot notify users individually of changes; we encourage users to review this policy periodically.
